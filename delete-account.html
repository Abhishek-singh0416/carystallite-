Delete Account & Data Policy

This policy applies to all mobile applications and websites developed and managed by Crystallite Infotech.

How to Request Account Deletion

Users who wish to delete their account and associated data may request deletion by following the steps below:

  1. Send an email to the official contact email mentioned below.
  2. Use the subject line: Request for Account Deletion.
  3. Mention the registered email address or mobile number used in the application.
Email: crystallite.in@gmail.com

Confirmation Before Deletion

Before proceeding with deletion, we confirm with the user that they fully understand that:

  • Account deletion is permanent
  • Deleted accounts cannot be recovered
  • All associated personal data will be erased

Data That Will Be Deleted

Once deletion is confirmed, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

  • User profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Login credentials
  • App usage data and history
  • Saved preferences and locations
  • Any personal data stored for app functionality

Data Usage & Security

Crystallite Infotech strictly follows a no personal data usage policy. User data is collected only for the user’s own application functionality and convenience.

  • We do not use user data for personal, marketing, or unauthorized purposes
  • We do not share user data with any third party
  • All stored data is securely encrypted
  • Our employees do not have direct access to sensitive personal data

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 working days after successful identity verification.

Legal & Compliance

All deletion requests and data handling processes are performed in accordance with applicable government laws, data protection rules, and regulatory guidelines.
