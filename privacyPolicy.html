Privacy Policy

Crystallite Infotech ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of our clients, users, and visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data across our websites, applications, and services.

1. Data Collection & Usage

We collect only the minimum data required to provide our services such as website development, application development, hosting, and related digital solutions. Any data collected is used strictly for legitimate business purposes.

2. No Data Sharing Policy

We follow a strict policy of not sharing any client or user data with any third party, agency, organization, or individual. Client data is never sold, rented, or transferred under any circumstances.

3. Government Compliance

All our applications, websites, and services are developed in full compliance with applicable Indian government laws, regulations, and data protection guidelines.

Wherever financial transactions are involved, we strictly follow government rules and ensure:

  • Proper invoicing for all purchases and sales
  • Transparent billing practices
  • Legal financial documentation

4. Data Security & Encryption

We take data security very seriously. All data collected by us is securely stored and encrypted. Our internal systems are designed so that sensitive data is not directly accessible to employees.

5. Employee Conduct & Ethics

Our employees and staff are bound by strict internal policies:

  • No use of offensive, abusive, or false language
  • Professional and respectful communication with all clients
  • Strict confidentiality of client data

6. Respect & Non-Discrimination Policy

Crystallite Infotech respects all nations, religions, cultures, beliefs, and communities. We strictly oppose and do not tolerate:

  • Racism or discrimination of any kind
  • Hate speech or offensive behavior
  • Promotion of illegal or unethical activities

7. Data Deletion Policy

Clients have full control over their personal data. If a client requests deletion of their data:

  • The request is honored immediately
  • All related data is permanently deleted from our databases
  • Deletion is completed within 24 hours of receiving the request

8. No Illegal Activities

We do not promote, support, or engage in any illegal activity. All products, services, websites, and applications developed by us align with government policies and legal standards.

9. Constitutional & Human Values

Our business is grounded in the principles of the Constitution, ethical conduct, and humanity. We respect governments, citizens, and legal systems across regions and countries.

10. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in laws or our practices. Any updates will be published on this page.
